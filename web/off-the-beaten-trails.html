Off the beaten trails

Hidden treasures and the discovery of the 'core' of this region as the one you have come to know through your grandparents' tales! Come with us to have a taste of the 'real' Sicily.

There are lots of reasons to head off the beaten trails, and Sicily is the perfect place to do it.  If you like walking in noiseless breathtaking landscapes, far from crowds and  tourist spots, this is where you can easily find what you are looking for.

You are likely to encounter  warm and welcoming people who are proud of their home and eager to show you how they live.

And last, but not least, once back home, you will be able to impress your friends and family with tales and pictures of places they have never seen elsewhere.

contact us for customized tours

The best way to explore Sicily and discover its wild beauties is walking. It is not always easy to follow the tracks, as they are not well signposted, BUT the scenery is rich in Nature, archaeology and breathtaking views! You'll walk on the Hills, cross the Rivers or walk Close to the Sea.

Discover these places following our tips and learn more about the interesting landmarks and beautiful spots worth visiting!

There are places where time seems to have stopped at a certain moment in the past, thus enabling you to watch with the same eyes of those who found their home here thousands of years ago: a safe place, naturally defensible from enemies, rich in resources: the perfect place where to settle a village, grow up one's children and transform the 'need of surviving' into a form of art.

These sites are nowadays hidden and not well   known, only fragments of that glorious past have survived to the present age, but exploring them is an amazing and unforgettable experience.

Everybody should  visit Sicily's old villages, located in dramatically  beautiful areas, out  of the touristy routes, where one can feel the most genuine soul of the island.

 There are unique small towns and villages which still preserve their ancient and incredibly breathtaking features: here we can find and admire men and women devoted to old  disappearing trades, and look for  special trattorias and food shops which offer a variety of local delicacies and the warm welcome of hospitable Sicilian people.
